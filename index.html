ACP is a quality measure, a revenue opportunity, and a moral imperative. So why isn't it happening?

The barriers aren't clinical. They're operational. ACP conversations take time physicians don't have, generate documentation that's hard to standardize, and the resulting wishes often can't be found when they matter most.

Time barrier

A meaningful ACP conversation takes 30-60 minutes. Most primary care visits are 15 minutes. The math doesn't work.

Documentation barrier

Handwritten forms, PDFs that live in filing cabinets, notes buried in EHR free text. When the ambulance arrives, nobody can find them.

Billing barrier

99497/99498 are some of the most under-billed codes in medicine. Practices leave millions on the table because the workflow doesn't exist.

Accessibility barrier

Even when an advance directive exists, it's rarely available at the point of care. 70% of directives never make it into the medical record.

CareGoals solves all four barriers.

AI-guided conversations that are clinically structured, automatically documented, legally valid, and instantly accessible via ComfortCard.
