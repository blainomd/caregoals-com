Everything in CareGoals

Sage conversations, values mapping, advance directive drafting, family sharing, and secure care team integration — all in one place.

smart_toy

Sage AI conversations

Voice or text. Sage asks open-ended questions, adapts to your answers, and builds on previous sessions. No scripts, no checkboxes — a real conversation at your pace.

favorite

Values mapping

Structured capture of what matters most — independence, dignity, connection, comfort. Values become the foundation for every medical and care preference that follows.

gavel

Advance directive drafting

State-specific directive templates generated from your Sage conversation. RON-ready (remote online notarization) in supported states. Your answers in, your legal document out.

person_check

Healthcare proxy designation

Designate your healthcare proxy and alternate, provide them with your care summary, and send a formal notification so the designation never comes as a surprise.

group

Family sharing

Share your care summary with designated family members. Control who sees what. Notify everyone at once or share selectively — your choice.

stethoscope

Care team integration

Share your summary directly with your physician or care team. For co-op.care members, your CareGoals summary is accessible to your care coordinators automatically.

memory

Cumulative memory

Sage remembers every previous session. When you return — months later, after a health change, after a family conversation — it builds on what you've already shared rather than starting from scratch.

lock

Secure and private

End-to-end encrypted. HIPAA-compliant infrastructure. Your data is never sold or shared without your explicit consent. You control your care summary completely.
